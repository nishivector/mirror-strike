# Mirror Strike

Reflect · Ricochet · Shatter

A precision reflex game where you control a mirror shield to deflect energy bolts into crystals. Time your reflections perfectly — shielded crystals require multiple hits, and bolts multiply as levels progress.

![Gameplay](screenshot.png)

## How to Play

- **Tap** to activate your mirror shield and deflect incoming bolts
- Angle your reflection to ricochet bolts into crystal targets
- Shielded crystals require multiple hits to shatter
- Clear all crystals to advance to the next level
- Don't let bolts pass without deflection!

## Levels

1. **Crystal Calm** — Single bolt, sparse crystals
2. **Dual Strike** — Two bolts, first shielded crystal appears
3. **Rapid Scatter** — Faster bolts, more shields
4. **Chaos Mirror** — Three bolts, precision required
5. **Total Fracture** — Maximum chaos, final challenge
